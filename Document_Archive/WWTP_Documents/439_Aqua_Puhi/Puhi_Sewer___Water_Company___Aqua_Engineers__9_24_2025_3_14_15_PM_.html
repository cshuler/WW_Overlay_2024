Puhi Sewer & Water Company is governed by the Public Utilities Commission (PUC) and is a subsidiary of Aqua Engineers, Inc, An Employee Owned Company.

Puhi Sewer & Water Company provides wastewater treatment services to certain areas of Puhi and Lihue.

New customers must complete the residential or industrial/commercial application at the right and submit the completed application with application fee to Puhi Sewer & Water Company prior to  contacting the Hawaii Department of Health for a permit.
